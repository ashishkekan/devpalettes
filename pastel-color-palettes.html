What Are Pastel Colors?

Pastel colors are soft, pale shades of colors created by adding white to pure hues. They have high lightness and low to medium saturation, giving them a gentle, soothing appearance.

These colors are perfect for creating calm, welcoming atmospheres in design. They're widely used in baby products, wedding invitations, spring-themed designs, and modern minimalist websites.

Popular Uses for Pastel Palettes

  • Wedding and event stationery
  • Children's product packaging
  • Beauty and cosmetic branding
  • Spring and Easter marketing campaigns
  • Minimalist web design backgrounds
