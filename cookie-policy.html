Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 2026

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to the website owners. At ColorPalettesHub, we use "Cookies" and similar technologies like "Local Storage" to provide you with a seamless experience.

Local Storage (Strictly Necessary)

To ensure ColorPalettesHub functions correctly without requiring user accounts, we utilize your browser's Local Storage. This data stays entirely on your device and is not sent to our servers. Based on our site functionality, we store the following:

  • Theme Preference (colorpallates-theme): We store your preference for "Dark" or "Light" mode so the site remembers your choice the next time you visit.
  • Saved Palettes & Settings (colorpallates-*): If you save a color palette or custom settings, this is stored locally so you don't lose your work when you refresh the page.

Note: Since this data is stored locally on your browser, clearing your browser cache will remove these settings.

Advertising Cookies (Google AdSense)

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. These cookies allow Google and its partners to serve ads to you based on your interests (Interest-Based Advertising).

  • These cookies track your visit and interaction with ads.
  • You can opt-out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

Third-Party Services

We use third-party services that may also set cookies on your device:

  • Google Fonts: Used for delivering fonts (Inter).
  • Font Awesome: Used for icons on the site.
  • CDN Services: Used to deliver scripts (Tailwind CSS) efficiently.

How to Manage Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can:

  • View what cookies are stored on your device.
  • Delete all or specific cookies.
  • Block cookies from specific sites or all sites.

Please note that blocking or deleting cookies, especially Local Storage data, may impact your experience on ColorPalettesHub (e.g., your saved palettes or theme settings may be lost).
