Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

At Dev Palettes, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines our data protection practices and your rights regarding the information stored when using our free color tools.

Our No-Server-Storage Promise

Your data stays with you. Dev Palettes does not use a backend database to store user information. All settings, saved palettes, and preferences are stored locally in your browser's Local Storage. We do not collect, sell, or transmit your personal data to third parties.

Introduction

Dev Palettes ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information when you use our free color tools.

Information We Collect

Our site is designed to minimize data collection. We primarily use Local Storage to save your settings directly on your browser. This data is never transmitted to our servers.

What We Store

Theme preferences, saved palettes, gradient settings.

What We DON'T Store

Personal names, emails, passwords, tracking IDs.

Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to deliver content and support our site:

  • Google Fonts: For typography (Inter).
  • Font Awesome: For icons.
  • Google AdSense: For advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

For more details on how cookies are used, please review our Cookie Policy.

Children's Privacy

Our service is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@devpalettes.com.
