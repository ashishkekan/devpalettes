Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

At Devpalettes, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines how we handle information when you use our tools, including cookies, analytics, and advertising services.

Our No-Server-Storage Promise

Your data stays with you. Devpalettes does not use a backend database to store user accounts. Many tool settings (like theme preferences) are stored locally in your browser. We do not ask you to create an account to use our tools.

Introduction

Devpalettes ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information may be processed when you use our website and tools.

Information We Collect

Our site is designed to minimize data collection. We primarily use Local Storage to save your settings directly on your browser. This data is never transmitted to our servers.

What We Store

Theme preferences, saved palettes, gradient settings.

What We DON'T Store

Personal names, emails, passwords, tracking IDs.

Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to deliver content and support our site:

  • Google Fonts: For typography (Inter).
  • Font Awesome: For icons.
  • Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand usage patterns (for example, which pages are popular) and improve the site.
  • Google AdSense: For advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

For more details on how cookies are used, please review our Cookie Policy.

Cookies & Your Choices

We use cookies and similar technologies for essential site behavior and, if you consent, to measure performance and advertising effectiveness. You can accept or reject non-essential cookies using our cookie consent banner.

If you previously accepted and later want to opt out, you can choose “Reject” the next time the banner appears, or clear site data in your browser settings.

GDPR / EEA Users

If you are located in the EEA/UK, you may have rights under applicable data protection laws, including the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data, and the right to withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@devpalettes.com.

Children's Privacy

Our service is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@devpalettes.com.
