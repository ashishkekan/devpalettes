What is a Color Palette Generator?

A Color Palette Generator is a tool used by designers and developers to create harmonious color schemes automatically. Instead of spending hours picking colors manually, you can generate endless combinations with a single click. This tool helps you find the perfect color scheme for your website, app, or graphic design project.

How to Use This Tool

  1. Generate: Press the "Spacebar" or click the Generate button to get new colors.
  2. Lock: Like a specific color? Click the lock icon to keep it while generating others.
  3. Copy: Click on any color block to copy its HEX code to your clipboard.
  1. Adjust Size: Add or remove colors to fit your design needs (min 3, max 8).
  2. Export: Export your palette as CSS variables, JSON object, or a PNG image.
  3. Save: Save your favorite palettes locally for future reference.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I export colors to CSS?

Click the "Copy CSS" button. This copies CSS variables (e.g., --color-1: #FF5733) to your clipboard, ready to paste into your stylesheet.

Can I save my palettes?

Yes! Click "Save Palette" to store it in your browser's local storage. You can reload it anytime from the "Saved Palettes" section.

Is this tool free?

Absolutely. This is a free tool for designers and developers with no usage limits.
