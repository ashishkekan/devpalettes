Designing with Dark Palettes

Dark color schemes have become increasingly popular in modern UI design. They reduce eye strain in low-light conditions, save battery on OLED screens, and provide a sleek, professional appearance.

Tips for Dark Mode Design

  • Avoid pure black (#000000); use dark grays instead for better depth
  • Ensure sufficient contrast for text accessibility (WCAG AA minimum)
  • Desaturate colors slightly to reduce eye strain
  • Use elevation shadows subtly to indicate hierarchy
