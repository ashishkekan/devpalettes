Tips for Choosing a Website Color Scheme

60-30-10 Rule

Use 60% primary color, 30% secondary, and 10% accent for balance.

Consider Contrast

Ensure text is readable against backgrounds. Use contrast checkers.

Know Your Audience

Different demographics react differently to colors.

Limit Your Palette

Stick to 2-4 colors to avoid visual clutter.
