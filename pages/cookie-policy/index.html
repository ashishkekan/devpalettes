Cookie Policy - Devpalettes

Last updated: March 2026

Devpalettes uses cookies and local storage to enhance your experience, save your preferences (like Dark Mode), and support our free tools through Google AdSense. This policy explains what data is stored and why.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to the website owners. At Devpalettes, we use "Cookies" and similar technologies like "Local Storage" to provide you with a seamless experience.

Local Storage (Strictly Necessary)

To ensure our tools function correctly without requiring user accounts, we utilize your browser's Local Storage. This data stays entirely on your device and is not sent to our servers. Based on our site functionality, we store the following:

devpalettes-theme Stores Dark/Light mode preference.
saved-palettes Saves user-generated color palettes.
gradient-state Saves current gradient settings.

Note: Clearing your browser cache will remove these settings.

Advertising Cookies (Google AdSense)

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits. You can opt-out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. Blocking cookies may impact your experience (e.g., your saved palettes may be lost).
