How to Add Particles to Your Website

Particle effects add a dynamic, modern feel to landing pages and portfolios. This generator creates pure CSS/JS particles without heavy libraries, ensuring fast load times.

  1. Adjust the sliders to set the count, size, and speed.
  2. Choose a color that matches your brand.
  3. Copy the generated HTML, CSS, and JS code.
  4. Paste it into your project file.
