What is Color Extraction?

Color extraction is the process of analyzing an image to identify its most prominent or visually significant colors. This helps designers create cohesive color palettes based on real-world inspiration, ensuring their designs match the mood and tone of the source image.

Extraction Methods

Dominant Colors

Finds the colors that cover the most area in the image. Perfect for understanding the overall color composition.

Vibrant Colors

Prioritizes highly saturated colors. Best for extracting punchy, eye-catching accents from photos.

How to Use This Tool

  1. Upload: Drag and drop an image or click to browse files.
  2. Configure: Choose the number of colors and extraction method.
  3. Extract: Click "Extract Colors" to generate your palette.
  4. Pick (Optional): Use the Color Picker tool to manually select specific pixels from the image.
  5. Export: Copy individual colors or export as CSS/JSON.
