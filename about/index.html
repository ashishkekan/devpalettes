Our Mission

Devpalettes was created with a simple goal: to make color selection accessible, enjoyable, and professional for designers, developers, and creators of all skill levels.

We believe that great design starts with great colors. Whether you're building a website, creating a brand identity, or designing a mobile app, choosing the right colors shouldn't be a barrier to your creativity.

Our Story

Founded by Ashish Kekaan, Devpalettes started as a passion project to solve a personal frustration with existing color tools. As a developer, Ashish noticed that many color generators were either too slow, cluttered with ads, or required sign-ups.

Built with a focus on speed and simplicity, the goal was to create a tool that just works—whether you need a quick HEX code or a complex gradient. Today, Devpalettes serves thousands of users daily, helping them create beautiful, accessible, and professional color schemes for their projects.

We are constantly improving and adding new features based on user feedback, staying true to our core mission: free, fast, and accessible color tools for everyone.
