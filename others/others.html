MacBook Pro M4 Max — Full Analysis

94
Editor's Choice
Best Creative Professional Laptop

After 3 weeks of daily use as my primary workstation, the MacBook Pro M4 Max has proven itself as the most capable laptop for creative professionals. The unified memory architecture with up to 128GB means workflows that previously required cloud computing can now happen locally.

The M4 Max chip delivers consistent multi-core performance without thermal throttling — something that's been a persistent issue with competitor alternatives. In our sustained Cinebench loop test, performance dropped less than 2% after 30 minutes.
