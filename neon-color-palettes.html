Using Neon Colors in Design

Neon colors are highly saturated, bright hues that mimic the glow of neon lights. They're perfect for creating bold, attention-grabbing designs with a futuristic or retro vibe.

Best Practices for Neon Palettes

  • Pair neon colors with dark backgrounds for maximum impact
  • Use sparingly as accents to avoid overwhelming users
  • Consider adding glow effects in CSS for authenticity
  • Test accessibility carefully—neon colors can be hard to read
  • Perfect for gaming, music, and tech brand identities
